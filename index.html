Udaipur city, also known as the 'City of Lakes', is a very popular tourist destination in Rajasthan.Udaipur is considered among the most romantic cities of India, Udaipur is a land around the azure water lakes, hemmed in by the lush hills of the Aravalis. The city is a blend of sights, sounds and experiences. With its kaleidoscope of fairy-tale palaces, lakes, temples, gardens and narrow lanes.Voted the Best City in the World in 2009 by Travel + Leisure magazine.Udaipur is now amongst the favourite wedding destinations for Indian as well as foreign nationals.

Education

Udaipur has a well-established education infrastructure. There are a number of universities, colleges and schools meeting the requirements of not only the city but the region and country as well. Udaipur is home to various government, deemed and private universities. Mohan Lal Sukhadia University, Maharana Pratap University of Agriculture and Technology and Rajiv Gandhi Tribal University are the major government universities in the city while J.R.N. Rajasthan Vidyapeeth is a deemed university. The IIM Udaipur,established in the city in 2011, was ranked as the fifth best management institute in the country by the Government of India,National Institutional Ranking Framework in 2016.

Smart City

Udaipur City has been selected as one of the Smart Cities under the Minister of Urban Development (MoUD) 100 smart city mission in India. The Municipal Corporation of Udaipur is preparing the smart city proposal for participate in the IInd phase of the smart city challenge and our city has to compete with others to make it to the top 20 to be taken up for the Ist round of funding, for the year 2015-16. The Municipal Corporation is requesting to the citizens of Udaipur for share their ideas, suggestion and solutions for transforming the Udaipur into the Smart City. The Citizens suggestions are very important for Udaipur City to develop as a Smart City in Rajasthan State.
