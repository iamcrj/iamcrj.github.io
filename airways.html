Airways

Maharana Pratap Airport

or known as Dabok Airport or Udaipur Airport is a commercial domestic airport situated 22 km east of Udaipur. Udaipur Airport is spread over 504 acres. The airport has one asphalt runway, oriented 08/26, 9000 feet/2743 metres long and 45 metres wide. Its 250 by 150 metre apron provides parking space for 3 Boeing 737s or Airbus A320 aircraft at a time. There are 4 check-in counters which can handle 600 passengers during peak time.

Airlines Destinations
Air India Delhi,Mumbai
Indigo Delhi,Mumbai and Hyderabad(via Delhi)
Jet Airways Bangalore(1 Stop),Delhi, Jaipur,Mumbai and Thiruvananthapuram(2 Stops)
