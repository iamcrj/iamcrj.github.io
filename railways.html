Railways

City Station

or Udaipur Railway Station or Main Station Udaipur City railway station is a railway station located in Udaipur, Rajasthan, India. The railway station is under the administrative control of North Western Railway of Indian Railways. Udaipur City Railway Station has 4 platforms and a total of 6 tracks. It is situated on the Udaipur Road, around 3 km from the city center, and around 20 km from Udaipur Airport.

Station Code : UDZ

Rana Pratap Nagar

railway station is the second railway station located in Udaipur, Rajasthan, India, besides the main Udaipur City railway station. The railway station is under the administrative control of North Western Railway of Indian Railways. Rana Pratap Nagar Railway Station has 2 platforms and has a Single Diesel BG track. It is situated in Khempura, near Thokar Chouraha, around 3 km from the city center, and around 18 km from Udaipur Airport. It main purpose of building this station was to decongest the main Udaipur City Railway Station.

Station Code : RPZ
